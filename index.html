Rachael Fairs Physio

  • Specialist Neurological Physiotherapist
  • Treatment in the comfort of your own home

As a neurological physiotherapist I specialise in treating individuals who are living with a long term condition, or who have suffered an injury/event, affecting their brain or spinal cord.

Whether you would like to maintain your independence, optimise your fitness, are keen to rebuild confidence in your movement, would like support to adjust to a new functional level, or would like to focus on improving your comfort/quality of life, I would be delighted to work with you on your rehabilitation journey.
